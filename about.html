About Us

Sanskar Bharti was founded in January 1981, in Lucknow. For last three decades, Sanskar Bharti has been tremendously known for its constant and consistent efforts to conserve, enhance and nurture Indian arts. Now we are the biggest organization country wide having more than 2000 units. Thousands of artists from various fields of art like music, theatre, literature, rangoli, sculpture, archeology, fine art are felt honoured and previliged in association of Sanskar Bharti.

Our Theme

Sanskar Bharti is not an entertaining society. We have never performed programmes that convey cheap entertainment consisted with vulgar & dirty messages. Sanskar Bharti strongly believe in positive creation of fine arts. Any stream of art leads to shape, beautify & uplift human being. Love, compassion & service for humanity take birth in a man art-associated. Art is a means to serve nation. Art teaches the devotion for God. Art inculcates the spirit of patriotism in the heart of countrymen. All streams of art in their peculiar way make us think positive, innovative and creative. Sanskar Bharti is bound to excel Indian sublime Values through art but we have been always open and eager to adopt new ideas that are relevant to our ideology.

Paribhasa

Sanskar Bharti wants a country where people feel free to improve their talent not just for name and fame but to serve humanity and worship God. We want a revival in everybody so that whole country may turn into ‘Anandmaya Bharat’. Sanskar Bharti monthly Magazine ‘Kalakunj Bharti’ has been delivering the essence of Indian arts and update information to its members for years. Not even in India Sanskar Bharti has its presence, Now globally in America, England, Mauritius, Thailand, Nepal etc.
